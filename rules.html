Quantum Connect Four - Game Rules

Objective:

The goal is to connect four of your coins horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Game Setup:

  • Player 0: Red
  • Player 1: Yellow
  • The bottom row starts with alternating red and yellow coins.

How to Play:

  1. Players take turns placing a coin or playing a gate secretly.
  2. Coin placements update the board instantly.
  3. Gate plays are not shown immediately.
  4. Each player has limited gates.

Number of Gates per Player:

  • 2 NOT Gates
  • 1 Hadamard Gate
  • 1 Swap Gate
  • 1 CNOT Gate
  • 1 Noise Gate

Gate Rules:

  • NOT Gate: Flips opponent's piece to yours next move.
  • Hadamard Gate: Adds randomness to the next move.
  • CNOT Gate: Conditional flip based on control column.
  • Swap Gate: Swap your piece with the opponent's.
  • Noise: Opponent's piece placed randomly next move.

Win Conditions:

  • Four in a Row: Connect four coins to win.
  • Draw: Board filled without a win.
